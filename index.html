Virtual Design Kit
VDK

VDK is a design software that allows you to create sheet metal constructions in virtual reality. Your own idea can be brought to life by snapping pieces together to create your own designs. VDK simultaneously generates the flat projection of your design, which serves as the stencil for laser cutting. A local company can then laser cut your design, and send it back to you in its flat state, ready to be folded together.

My way

After my apprenticeship as a chemical laboratory assistant at the University of Bern, I worked for several years in forensic molecular biology at the Institute of Forensic Medicine at the University of Bern. Although I love working in the laboratory, I am also drawn to design. That's why I decided to break out of my line of work and start a study of postindustrial design at Hyperwerk. My studies allow me to pursue my individual interests. These lead me from 3D modeling and animation to virtual reality work.
