Immersive Art

During the first lockdown in 2020, many museums could be visited virtually. I don't want to reproduce reality but create an experience that would otherwise not be possible. The concept: experience paintings playfully and immerse yourself directly in them. In this way, details and painting techniques can be discovered for oneself in an interactive way.

Virtual Design Kit - VDK

VDK is a design software that allows you to create sheet metal constructions in virtual reality. Your idea can be brought to life by snapping pieces together to create your designs. VDK simultaneously generates the flat projection of your design, which serves as the stencil for laser cutting. A local company can then laser cut your design, and send it back to you in its flat state, ready to be folded together.
